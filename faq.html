General

Question: What is azChat?

azChat is a messaging app, simple and free. You can use azChat on your devices at the same time — your messages sync seamlessly across any number of your phones, tablets or computers.

Right now with azChat, you can send only messages, but we are working on sending photos and other files types.

Question: Who it is for?

azChat is for everyone loves chatting. Business users and small teams may like the channels feature like one in Slack app.

Question: How it is different from Slack?

Thanks to our location in Belarus, azChat is faster and way more secure. On top of that, azChat is free and will stay free — no ads, no subscription fees, forever.

Question: How old is azChat?

Developing of azChat for macOS was started on December, 2017. The alpha version of azChat for iOS officially launched on February, 2018. More and more azChat clients are going to appear in the near future.

Question: Is it available on my device?

The answer is yes, because if you are reading this page right now, you can use our Web version of azChat. Yet you can use azChat on smartphones, tablets, and even computers. We have apps for iOS 9+ and macOS 12.10+.

Question: Where is azChat based?

The azChat development team is based in Minsk.

The only one azChat developer come from Minsk, the city famous for its great universities. Currently we have only one server located in BSUIR dormitory № 4.
